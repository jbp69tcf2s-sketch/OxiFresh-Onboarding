💬 Communication Standards

At RBS, communication is everything. As a VA, you speak on behalf of our clients and our company. Every word you write reflects on the business. These standards are non-negotiable.

✉️ Email Etiquette

Always use a professional greeting and sign-off. Match the client's existing email tone (formal vs. friendly). Subject lines should be clear and action-oriented. Proofread every email before sending. Use proper grammar — no slang, abbreviations, or emoji in professional emails.

⏱️ Response Time Standards

Acknowledge all emails within 2–4 hours during work hours, even if just to say "I've received this and will follow up by [time]." Never leave a message unanswered for more than 24 hours. Urgent flagged messages must be acknowledged within 1 hour.

📝 Status Updates

Proactively update your supervisor on task progress. If you're stuck on something, say so early — don't wait until a deadline. Use the format: "I'm working on [task]. ETA is [time]. I need [resource] to complete it."

Situation Best Channel Response Time
Urgent issue needing decision Phone / WhatsApp call Immediate
Quick question or status update WhatsApp / Slack message Within 1 hour
Task delivery or report Email with attachment Per deadline
Client external communication Email (professional tone) 2–4 hours
SOP or document submission Shared Google Drive / Email Per schedule
Team check-in or coaching Google Meet / Zoom Scheduled

✅ Always DO

  • Proofread before sending anything
  • Use the client's correct name and title
  • Confirm receipt of important messages
  • Set clear deadlines when you give ETAs
  • Copy the right people on emails
  • Keep a professional tone always

❌ Never DO

  • Ignore messages or go silent
  • Reply when angry or flustered
  • Overpromise and underdeliver
  • Share confidential info casually
  • Use slang or informal language with clients
  • Make commitments without checking first

📋 Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

These are the core SOPs you will follow daily as the Oxi Fresh NE Florida Admin VA. Read through each one carefully and refer back to this section whenever you need guidance. Always follow the steps exactly as written unless instructed otherwise by Joy or Sean.

📌

Important Reminder

These SOPs are your daily reference guide. If you are ever unsure about a step — stop, refer to this guide, and ask Joy before proceeding. Active listening and taking notes are essential.

⭐ SOP 1 — Customer Ratings Management

Objective: Track customer ratings and encourage Google reviews for high ratings.

1 Check GMB Ratings

  • Log into the GMB account and review recent ratings
  • Record ratings in the 2024 Customer Satisfaction Ratings worksheet

2 Send SMS Requests for Ratings

  • Access DialPad and retrieve customer contact numbers from CRM job reports (yesterday's completed jobs)
  • Send SMS: "Thank you for using Oxi Fresh! Please rate your experience from 5 (Best) to 1 (Poor) by responding to this text. Text 'stop' to quit."
  • Exclude customers who already left GMB ratings
  • Monitor replies in the work email inbox and record in the worksheet

3 Send Google Review Requests & Follow-Ups

  • For 5-star SMS customers → send Google review link via CRM (city-specific link)
  • 1st follow-up: 3–5 days after initial request
  • 2nd follow-up: 10–14 days after 1st follow-up
  • Monthly SMS: for any 5-star customer who still hasn't left a review
  • Listen360: for customers who rated 10, request a 5-star Google review

⏱️ SOP 2 — Technician Timekeeping

Objective: Monitor technician punctuality for their first job of the day using GPS Trackit.

📍 Steps

  • Log into GPS Trackit and check technician STOP time for their first job
  • 3 Points — 15+ minutes early
  • 2 Points — On time to 14 minutes early
  • 1 Point — 1–15 minutes late
  • 0 Points — More than 15 minutes late
  • Record points in the 2024 Customer Satisfaction Ratings worksheet

📞 SOP 3 — Call Quotes Management

Objective: Follow up on customer quotes and increase booking conversions.

📋 Follow-Up Schedule

  • 1st Attempt: 1–2 days after quote — quick check-in while fresh
  • 2nd Attempt: 7–10 days later — reconnect and offer assistance
  • 3rd Attempt: 3–4 weeks later — customer may be ready now
  • 4th Attempt: 2 months after quote — check if timing is better
  • 5th Attempt: End of 3 months — quote expiration reminder (final)
  • If booked → record in VA Appointment Booked worksheet
  • After 5th attempt → mark as completed, no response, not interested, or expired in CRM

🎯 SOP 4 — LSA Lead Management & Validation

Objective: Validate leads, manage potential customers, and track all LSA activity.

✅ Valid vs. Invalid Leads

  • Valid: Requested carpet cleaning, within service area, showed legitimate interest
  • Invalid (Dispute): Spam/robocall, wrong number, duplicate, outside service area, unrelated service, immediate hang-up
  • To dispute → open lead in LSA dashboard → click "Dispute Lead" → select reason → add notes → track status

🔄 Lead Lifecycle Flow

  • New Lead → Contacted → Quoted → Scheduled/Booked → Completed
  • Or → Cancelled → Voided / Expired
  • All leads must be updated daily in the LSA tracking worksheet
  • No lead should remain open beyond 90 days

📊 Quote Follow-Up Schedule

  • Same Day → Send estimate and thank customer
  • 24 Hours → 1st follow-up call/text
  • 3 Days → 2nd follow-up
  • 7 Days → 3rd follow-up
  • 14 Days → Courtesy follow-up
  • 30 Days → Monthly follow-up if still interested
  • Monthly → Continue light follow-up until 90-day expiration

📝 SOP 5 — Work Order Management

Objective: Process completed work orders and validate payments.

💳 Payment Processing Steps

  • Credit Card: Confirm in Clover → update & complete CRM work order → save in Pronto Form folder
  • Cash: Update CRM → record in For Invoice and CASH Payment worksheet → wait for Sean to confirm before marking complete
  • Invoice: Email invoice to client → save in worksheet → convert invoice in CRM to update job status
  • Technician Errors: Return work order via Live Pronto Form → SMS technician with corrections needed → re-review once resubmitted

📅 SOP 6 — Call Past Customers (9–12 Months)

Objective: Reconnect with past customers and offer annual carpet cleaning services.

📞 Follow-Up Timeline

  • 9-Month Call: Retrieve customer list from CRM export → call and offer annual cleaning → record in Call Logs worksheet
  • Follow-Up #1: 2–4 weeks after 9-month call (for unreached customers)
  • Follow-Up #2: 6–8 weeks after 9-month call
  • At 12 Months: Close out as Booked / Not Interested / No Response / Requested Future Callback

📊 SOP 7 — Monthly Reports

Objective: Compile and submit required monthly reports.

📈 Reports to Prepare

  • Market Share Report: Export CRM data → fill Monthly tab (jobs, revenue, KPIs), Marketing Codes tab, and Zip Codes tab
  • Closed Sales Report: Export CRM closed sales data → populate Closed Sales worksheet
  • Technician Performance Summary: Open Performance Summary file → review scorecards (job completion, satisfaction, punctuality) → distribute to Sean
  • Cancellation Logs: Export canceled jobs from CRM → fill Cancellation Logs worksheet with customer name, job date, and reason

🤝 SOP 8 — BNI Referral Tracking & Management

Objective: Track all BNI referrals received and given, ensure follow-up, scheduling, and accurate reporting.

🔄 BNI Steps

  • Referrals Received: Log into BNI Connect App → check Referrals Received → call customer immediately → book job using BNI campaign code → enter revenue in BNI Referral Tracker → mark as "Got the Business"
  • Referrals Given: Listen for customer needs during calls → gather name, phone, address, service needed → submit via BNI App → log in tracker
  • Monthly Review: Verify all referrals have accurate dollar amounts, are marked complete, and updated in both the tracker and BNI system
  • Dispositions: Got The Business / No Response / Not Interested / Unable to Contact / Did Not Get the Business
